Meet Veronica

Long ago and far away, in the magical land of Toronto, Canada, lived a young woman named Veronica, and her two daughters. One day, under the most serendipitous of circumstances, she met her best friend and the man who would one day become her husband. Later, they left the snowy wonderland up in the Great White North and made the move to Portland, Oregon. In 2013, they added to their family (Hi, Levi!) and in 2016, grew their family again when their dog Rosco was added to the mix.

For the last five years, Veronica has worked as a freelance creative professional in one capacity or another. In additional to her work as a front-end developer, Veronica currently owns and operates her photography studio, Veronica Gillas Photography. She is a self-proclaimed hamburger connoisseur, works best with an almond milk latte in her hand, loves the beach, tickle fights with her kids, date nights with her husband, and Blue Star Donuts.
